About Our Organization

College Prep at Penn Charter was developed by educators from William Penn Charter School and Simon Gratz High School. In 1971 Penn Charter teacher Joe Perrot was inspired to call Marcus Foster, the principal of Simon Gratz High School on Hunting Park Avenue. Recognizing the close proximity of the two schools, he proposed a program that would make Penn Charterâ€™s resources available to Gratz students who wanted to pursue college. Since that time, College Prep has hosted students from Gratz and other neighborhood public high schools each summer for a program that develops their skills and supports their aspirations. College Prep students are exclusively from Philadelphia public and charter schools. In the past 48 years College Prep has worked with over 1,000 students in Philadelphia, many of whom have spent two summers committed to the program.
